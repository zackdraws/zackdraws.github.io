About Me

Hello, my name is Zack Johnson. I'm a passionate Animator and Storyboard Artist based in Irvine, California. I graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Animation from the Laguna College of Art and Design. My education provided me with a solid foundation in traditional and digital animation techniques.

Experience

Over the years, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, ranging from animated TV series to marketing campaigns. I've collaborated with talented individuals and learned valuable skills along the way.

Interests

When I'm not animating, you can find me exploring new art galleries, watching classic movies, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. I'm also an avid reader and enjoy diving into a good book whenever I get the chance.
