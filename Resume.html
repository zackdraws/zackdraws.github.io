About Me

PDF Copy of my Resume

My name is Zack Johnson, I am a Designer, Animator and Storyboard Artist based in Irvine, California. From an early age, I've always been interested in animation, design, and art in general.

In 2020, I received my BFA in Character Animation from the Laguna College of Art and Design. Since then, I've worked on short films, commercial animation projects, as well as teaching at the YMM Art Space and The Artist Lab in Irvine. You can find more of my work here at ZackDraws.com, and feel free to contact me at ZackJohnsonArt@gmail.com.

Thank you, Zack Johnson
